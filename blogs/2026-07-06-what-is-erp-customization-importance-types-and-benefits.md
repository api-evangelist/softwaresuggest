ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) systems are designed to streamline business operations by bringing finance, inventory, sales, procurement, HR, and other functions into a single platform. Yet, every business operates differently, and standard ERP features cannot always support unique workflows, industry-specific requirements, or compliance demands. This is where ERP customization plays a critical role.
