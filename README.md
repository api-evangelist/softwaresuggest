# SoftwareSuggest

SoftwareSuggest (https://www.softwaresuggest.com/) is a business software discovery and recommendation platform that helps businesses, organizations, and professionals find the right software solutions. Founded in 2014 and headquartered in Ahmedabad, India, SoftwareSuggest is operated by AppItSimple Infotek Pvt. Ltd. The platform covers over 800 software categories, lists more than 50,000 software products, and hosts over 40,000 verified user reviews. Top competitors include G2, Capterra, TrustRadius, and Clutch.

## Platform Features

SoftwareSuggest enables buyers to search, filter, and compare business software side by side across categories including CRM, HR management, accounting, project management, and marketing automation. Expert consultants assist buyers in narrowing down options based on their specific requirements. Vendors can create free product listings or purchase paid plans to increase visibility through premium placements, PPC campaigns, MQL services, and branded content solutions.

## Vendor Portal

Software vendors can list their products at https://www.softwaresuggest.com/vendors. The vendor portal at https://www.softwaresuggest.com/vendorsportal provides a dashboard for managing listings, tracking leads, and accessing performance analytics. Listing guidelines are published at https://www.softwaresuggest.com/vendors/guidelines. Products must primarily serve B2B purposes and be pre-built, off-the-shelf packaged solutions to qualify.

## Affiliate Program

SoftwareSuggest operates a CPL-based affiliate program at https://www.softwaresuggest.com/affiliates. Affiliates earn commissions by placing links or banners that drive verified leads to the platform. Payouts are processed monthly with a minimum threshold of $100.

## APIs

SoftwareSuggest does not currently publish a public developer API. The platform appears in the Datarade data marketplace (https://datarade.ai/data-providers/softwaresuggest/profile) as a data provider, but no formal API documentation or public endpoints have been disclosed. Vendors and partners seeking programmatic integration should contact SoftwareSuggest directly through the vendor portal.
