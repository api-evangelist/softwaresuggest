# SoftwareSuggest

SoftwareSuggest is a business software discovery and recommendation platform that helps organizations find, compare, and select the right software solutions for their needs. Founded in 2014 by AppItSimple Infotek Pvt. Ltd. and headquartered in Ahmedabad, India, the platform covers over 800 software categories, lists more than 50,000 software products, and hosts over 40,000 verified user reviews. The platform serves both software buyers looking to evaluate and purchase solutions and software vendors seeking qualified leads and market visibility. SoftwareSuggest does not currently publish a public developer API but operates as a data provider via marketplaces such as Datarade.

**URL:** [SoftwareSuggest](https://www.softwaresuggest.com/)

## Platform Features

SoftwareSuggest enables buyers to search, filter, and compare business software side by side across categories including CRM, HR management, accounting, project management, and marketing automation. Expert consultants assist buyers in narrowing down options based on their specific requirements. Vendors can create free product listings or purchase paid plans to increase visibility through premium placements, PPC campaigns, MQL services, and branded content solutions.
